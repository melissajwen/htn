TRACKR

Automatically identify blacklisted persons through your security system.

WHAT WE DO

We use state of the art face recognition technology to automatically scan and filter faces in security camera footage that belong to wanted criminals and other blacklisted persons. By comparing these faces with the photos in our database, we will be able to help find these people faster.

The system will alert a point of contact (whether it be an emergency contact, the police, or yourself) when a potential match shows up in our system. It will automatically send snapshots of the footage as well as details about when and where they were seen.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

In light of recent acts of terrorism worldwide, we want to take advantage of a security system already in place to help safeguard public spaces. Our software requires minimal setup and interference and is intuitive to use. Knowing when a potentially dangerous person is within your vicinity can help you and others take the appropriate measures to ensure the safety of all.
